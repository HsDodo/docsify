### <font  size=3px color='#8d4bbb'>建议信 </font>

 <font size=4em color='#9ed048'>建议句型:</font> 

 	1. it is highly suggested that you do something
 	2. it is advisable for you to do something
 	3. it would be better if you could do something
 	4. you are suggested/expected/supposed to do
 	5. you could do worse than do sth 你不妨做

 <font size=4em color='#9ed048'>正文布局:</font> 

​	Dear Sir or Madam, （注意要加逗号,不加逗号会扣分）

​    第一段:  说明问题 再说要提建议

​	第二段: 引出建议

 The following are some measures that,I reckon can be taken...

1. first and foremost / firstly
2. in addition / what's more / moreover / furthermore / additionally

​    第三段: 结尾表示希望建议有用，要是比较正式的话要记得感谢

​	例:

1. Hopefully  you  will  find  my  suggestion constructive  and  thanks  for  your  time and  attention  to  this  letter.

2. I  hope  you  will  find  the  proposals  above  useful  and  constructive  ......

1. 柱状图，饼柱图考的多

 	①句：引出话题 ( 20 words)

​     ③句：描述数据 ( 20 words)

__第二段__

 	①句：主旨句( 20 words)

​	 ②句：要点一( 25 words)

​     ③句：要点二 ( 25 words)

（切入角度：原因、意义或影响）

经济、科技、政策、观念、教育、就业、交通、



__第三段__ （两句）总结+建议或预测

​	 ①句：总结观点( 20 words)

​	 ②句：提出建议( 25 words)



<font  size=3px color='#8d4bbb'>图表必备词汇 </font>

图表：chart , graph

柱状图：bar chart 、column chart

饼状图：pie chart

线条图：line chart 、curve chart

表格：table

数据：number、figure、data、statistics

比例：percentage、proportion、share、portion、rate

显示：show、reveal、illustrate、demonstrate、describe、indicate、reflect

上升：increase、rise、climb、grow、go upwards、ascent、mount up

剧增：boom、explode、soar、surge、jump、rocket(up)、shoot up

下降：decrease、decline、descend、fall、drop、diminish、shrink 、go downwards

骤降：slump、collapse、crash、slash

保持稳定：keep/stay/remain/maintain+stabl/steady/constant/unchanged

占比例：account for 、count for 、make up、contribute to 、constitute

快速的、剧烈的：rapid （rapidly）、sharp（sharply）、dramatic（dramatically）、significant（significantly）、sudden（suddenly）、marked（markedly）、remarkable（remarkedly）、noticeable（noticeably）、fast（fast），great（greatly）

缓慢的：slow（slowly）、gradual（gradually）、smooth（smoothly）、slight（slightly）

大约：about、nearly、around、roughly、some、approximately
